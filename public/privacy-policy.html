Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to MY LIFE & MY CASHFLOW AFFIRMATIONS ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

When you use the App, we collect:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and password (securely hashed) when you create an account
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, including booklets viewed and affirmations accessed
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for analytics purposes
  • Payment Information: Transaction data for booklet purchases (processed through secure payment methods)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Provide affirmation content and booklet access
  • Process payments and booklet purchases
  • Improve the App's functionality and user experience
  • Send important account notifications

Data Security

Your data security is important to us. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion. All passwords are hashed and stored securely. Payment information is processed through secure payment gateways.

Data Storage

Your account data and usage information are stored on secure servers hosted by reliable third-party providers. You can delete your account at any time through the App settings, which will remove your personal data from our systems.

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services for:

  • Payment processing
  • Cloud storage and database services
  • Analytics (to improve the App)

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing their use of your data.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If we discover that we have collected such information without consent, we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and notifying you through the App.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through the App's support section.
